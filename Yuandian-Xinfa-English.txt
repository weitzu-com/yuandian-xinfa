Yuandian Xinfa
(The Heart Method of the Origin Point)

Set your aspirations in accord with the Tao, follow the laws of nature,
Through diligent study and inner light, the heart perceives its true nature,
Peace and safety are the greatest blessings; build and nurture the nation,
Cultivate the self, rectify the heart, and act with utmost sincerity,
Supreme goodness is like water; when it flows freely, channels form of themselves,
Dragons soar and tigers leap, all gathering together as one,
Virtue spreads across the world — this is the true China.

Wei Qinguang
May 14, 2025, 1:13 AM, Dongying, Shandong
